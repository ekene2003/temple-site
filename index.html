About Us

Your Trusted Partner in Oil & Gas Logistics and Engineering

Falcon Point Service Limited is a Nigerian-based company delivering a full suite of oil and gas support services, with a strong presence across West Africa. We specialize in vessel management, offshore logistics, civil and engineering works, and global cargo transport. Our team combines extensive experience with a deep understanding of both international standards and local operating conditions. Whether managing complex offshore operations or ensuring timely cargo delivery, we are committed to achieving superior outcomes safely, efficiently, and in full regulatory compliance. We pride ourselves on our operational integrity, commitment to safety, and a hands-on approach that ensures every project meets client expectations and industry benchmarks.

Why Choose Falcon Point?

Falcon Point Services has developed into a reliable provider of marine, vessel management, and engineering services in Nigeria. Each year, we manage over three vessels through a professional network that spans all ports in Nigeria and across West Africa. Our marine and vessel management operations combine local expertise with international standards to deliver high-quality, proactive service backed by well-defined processes that meet client expectations. Our business systems are aligned with ISO standards, with a strong commitment to continuous improvement in both the quality and scope of services offered. At Falcon Point Services, we place a strong emphasis on fostering a visible Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE) culture throughout the organization—prioritizing the safety of our staff, vessel crews, and all personnel under our responsibility.
